Instructions Optimize About

Step 1: Guild Bonuses

  • Guild bonuses can only be accessed if you are in a guild.
    (Leave input field at 0% if you are not part of a guild)
  • Guild Bonuses can be found in the Guild House under the Research tab.
    (City > Guild > Guild House > Research)
  • Type the Current Lvl stat increase for each of the 4 stats in their corresponding input fields in the optimizer.

Step 2: Collection Percentages

  • Some people may have collection slots locked.
    (If you have a locked slot leave the corresponding input field at 0%)
  • Collection Percentages can be found in the Hero tab under the Collection I and II tabs.
    *Must press the Upgrade Slots button to see the percentages.
    (Hero > Collection I) & (Hero > Collection II)
  • For every slot, type the percentage number onto the corresponding input field in the optimizer.

Step 3: Equipped Gear

  • Equipped gear can be found in the Hero tab.
    (Hero)
  • Get the names of all your equipped gear and select them in the corresponding dropdown selection field.

Step 4: Collection Gear

  • Your collection pieces can be found in the same location as the collection percentages.
    (Hero > Collection I) & (Hero > Collection II)
  • Get the name of every gear piece you have and select them in the corresponding dropdown selection field along with their current awake level and boost level.
    (If you have locked slots look for "Locked Slot" in the corresponding dropdown selection field and leave other fields alone)

    • Awake Level:

    • ★★★★★ = 0
    • ★ = 1
    • ★★ = 2
    • ★★★ = 3
    • ★★★★ = 4
    • ★★★★★ = 5

    Boost Level:

    • This is a number that can be seen on the top right of gear pieces
    • If there isn't a number then the boost level for the piece is 1
    • If there is a number than copy that number onto the associated boost level input

Once everything has been inputed just press the optimize button in the opimizer and watch the magic happen.

Note: This Optimizer only uses Legenday Gear Pieces.
